About us

Making Your Vision Come Alive

Salama Engineering Industries is a trusted name in building high-quality heat transfer equipment and serves as the exclusive distributor of TRANTER (Global) Plate Heat Exchangers in Saudi Arabia. The company that began in Dammam has a long history of success and creativity.

Decades of Experience Across Key Industries

Having more than 20 years of experience in the field, Salama Engineering Industries is the leading firm that has been involved in the designing, developing, and manufacturing heat exchangers for various industries.

State-of-the-Art Facilities and Skilled Workforce

In Eastern Province, we operate three modern R & D facilities. One of them is focused on the manufacturing and servicing of process equipment while the others are dedicated to the fabrication and coating of HVAC-R coils.

Comprehensive Engineering Solutions

Our company develops, manufactures, and services heat exchangers coolers as well as HVAC coils. Industrial applications can range from basic to intricate designs.

Pipe Spool Fabrication

Pipe Spool Fabrication

Our area of expertise lies in the production of pipe spool fabrication of different sizes, shapes, and designs for use in different industries including Oil, Gas, Petrochemical, Chemical, and Water.

Our certified pipeline personnel are skilled in a wide range of welding specifications and materials. With expertise across various welding processes, our team can efficiently perform techniques such as SMAW, TIG, and MIG welding organically.

Pressure Vessels

Pressure Vessels

We provide high pressure vessels built to industry best practices and safety levels to cater for various clients in the oil, gas and petrochemical industries. We offer a full package design (ASME SEC VIII Division 1 and 2, ASME SEC II), detailed engineering, manufacture and fabrication both in horizontal and vertical configurations.

Our range of pressure vessel includes:

  • Air Receivers
  • Separators, Receivers and Condensers
  • Knock Out Drums
  • Production Saturation Traps (High Pressure)
  • Scrubber Drums
  • De-Cock Drums
View Details
Condenser Coils

Condenser Coils

We specialize in designing and manufacturing high-performance condenser coils using a variety of fin and tube materials, available in multiple thicknesses. Our core materials include copper, aluminum, and pre-coated aluminum fins.

Our products are meant to be used in different applications, so we offer coils compatible with different refrigerants (Condenser and Evaporator coil), liquids (Chilled water/glycol and Hot water/glycol coils), and fluids (Steam and Air charge coils).

Fabricated Tanks

Fabricated Tanks

Our modern in-house engineering and fabrication workshop ensures that we provide high-quality, bespoke storage tanks to your precise requirements. There are also other materials available such as stainless steel, carbon steel, alloy metals, and aluminum alloys that make our tanks safe for one-time or repeated uses.

Industries that seek our solutions include Oil & Gas, Petrochemicals, Power, Chemicals, Plastics, and many others.

Our range of tanks available in the region includes:

  • Flat Bottom or Sloping Bottom Tanks with Conical or Flat Heads
  • Silos & Hoppers
  • Rectangular Tanks

We deliver top-quality, reliable tank solutions that go beyond the standards of the industry and our customers’ requirements.
