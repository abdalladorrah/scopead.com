About Us

Since 2009

Oma Touch Trading was born in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia in 2009, focusing on importing and wholesaling household items, antiques, and gifts. We started from scratch, relying on the trust of our customers. Within a short period, we began manufacturing our ideas and, by the grace of God, achieved our goals. We aim to be among the leaders in our field, distributing our products across the Gulf and the Arab and Islamic world.

Our Goals

We aim to achieve and maintain a leading position in our business field, both within Saudi Arabia and the Gulf region, and to be distinguished among our clients.

Our Vision

We strive to develop and create ideas and environments filled with enthusiasm and challenge. We are satisfied with what we have achieved, thanks to God's grace, in our relationships with our suppliers and customers. We have set our goals and plans for many years with renewed ideas.

Why Choose Oma Touch Trading

We offer the best home products with high quality and competitive prices. Our commitment to customer satisfaction sets us apart in the market. The trust of our customers is the foundation of our success.
