About Us

Al-Sadiq Al-Amin Academy is a beacon for teaching the Book of Allah, dedicated to sisters who wish to memorize the Quran and correct their recitation. We offer a stimulating online educational environment, completely free of charge.

  • Quran memorization with Tajweed rules.
  • Recitation correction and continuous revision.
  • Qualified and experienced female teachers.

We strive to connect hearts with the Book of Allah and facilitate its memorization and reflection for every sister desiring good. Join us to be among the people of the Quran, in circles that suit your time and level.
